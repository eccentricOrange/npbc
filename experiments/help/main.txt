usage: npbc <command> [args]

    calculate [-m <month>] [-y <year>] [-p "<missing papers>"]
    calculate, save, and copy the bill for a given month
        -m, --month:    specify the month to calculate for
        -y, --year:     specify the year to calculate for
        -p, --papers:   specify papers to calculate for, in the format:
                        paper_1:day_range11,...,day_range1n;...;paper_m:day_rangem1,..,day_rangemn

    editpapers
    get taken to an interactive interface for editing newspapers

    -h, --help
    display help
